ABOUT

Alvin Man is a multi-talented singer, songwriter, musician and dancer. He got into music at the young age of 5, and has never looked back. Influenced by a diverse blend of genres including Pop, R&B, Rock, and Hip Hop, Alvin offers a unique sound that has captivated audiences wherever he has performed.

In 2009, Alvin became the winner of the New Talent Singing Awards, a renowned Chinese singing competition in the city of Vancouver. He went on to represent Vancouver in the International Chinese New Talent Singing Awards held in Hong Kong.

Alvin recently won the SQ17 Songwriters Quest competition with his song, 我們的力量 / You'll Make It.

He is also the lead-vocalist, keyboardist, and songwriter for the band Chasing Eidolon, who are currently working on new songs for their second album, release date TBA.
